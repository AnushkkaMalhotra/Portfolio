COOKBOOK

This cookbook is a comprehensive guide for beginners eager to explore the vibrant and flavorful world of Korean cuisine. Designed with a visually engaging aesthetic, the book employs a playful combination of fonts like Impact, AM Udine, and Raleway to capture attention and create a modern, approachable feel. Complementing its appealing design are high-quality, mouthwatering images of iconic dishes that are sure to inspire and excite readers.
